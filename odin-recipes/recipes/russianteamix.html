Russian Tea Mix

This recipe was passed down to me by my Grandmother (who isn't Russian)


Ingredients

  • 2 cups "Tang"-orange flavored instant breakfast drink mix (enough for 8 quarts)
  • 1 large package instant lemonade mix (enough for 2 quarts)
  • 1 cup instant tea
  • 1+1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon cloves

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients
  2. Mix well
  3. Store in an airtight container
  4. To serve, stir 2 heaping teaspoons of mix into 1 cup of hot water
  5. Makes about 4 cups of mix
  6. Enjoy!
